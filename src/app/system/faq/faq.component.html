What projects can I do on Workifest?

Popular jobs include web, mobile, software development, design, writing, and more.

How does Workifest make money?

Freelancers are charged a 2% service fee on their earnings.

What’s the Top Star Program?

Recognition for freelancers who consistently deliver outstanding work.

Is Workifest free to join?

Yes, it’s free to join, create a profile, and search for work. A 2% freelancer service fee applies to earnings.

How does Workifest help in career growth?

Guidance and tips are provided to help freelancers build successful careers.

What can I gain from Workifest?

Work with great clients and well-known brands. Get support and guidance to boost success.

Is Workifest good for building a business?

Yes, you can create an agency and collaborate as a service provider.

What projects are available on Workifest?

Opportunities across various industries for both short-term and long-term contracts.

What are Loyalty Coins?

Coins are used to view jobs and submit proposals. Free coins are given at registration.

What do the different recognitions mean?

Recognition levels include Rising Star, Top Star, Top Mega Star, and Expert, based on work performance and feedback.
